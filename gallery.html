Reading is a cornerstone of intellectual growth and personal development. It opens doors to new worlds, perspectives, and ideas, fostering empathy, critical thinking, and creativity. Through books, one can gain knowledge, expand vocabulary, and enhance communication skills. Moreover, reading cultivates a lifelong love for learning and equips individuals with the tools to navigate an increasingly complex world. Whether delving into fiction to explore the depths of human experience or diving into non-fiction to grasp new concepts and insights, reading is essential for both individual flourishing and societal progress.

First Lie Win

First Lies Wins is a 2024 psychological thriller by Ashley Elston. The novel follows Evie Porter, a con artist hired by a mysterious employer to infiltrate the life of a handsome businessman. Evie sinks deeper into this false life, and the job slowly starts to spiral out of her control.

Bright Young Women

Bright Young Women tells the story of two women brought together by horror and tragedy, and is based on real-life murders that occurred in America in the 1970s. Tina Cannon is trying to find out what happened to her friend Ruth, who disappeared in broad daylight from a crowded beach near Seattle.
